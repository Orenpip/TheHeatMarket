Compass — Project Summary                                                                            
                                                                                                       
  What it is: A web app for high school students to track their personal growth through periodic       
  reflections. A counselor/admin writes curated reflection templates; students complete them over the  
  year and the app generates a "signal profile" showing patterns in their interests, character, and    
  trajectory.                                                                                          
                                                                                                     
  ---                                                                                                  
  Tech Stack                                                                                           
                                                                                                       
  - Frontend/Backend: Next.js 14 (app router), hosted on Vercel                                        
  - Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL), Prisma ORM with WASM client + pg driver adapter                   
  - Auth: Clerk (roles: student, counselor, admin via publicMetadata)                                  
  - AI: Anthropic SDK directly in Next.js — claude-sonnet-4-6 for synthesis, claude-haiku-4-5 for      
  prompts                                                                                              
  - Monorepo: pnpm workspaces + Turborepo (apps/web, packages/db)
  ---
  Core User Flow
  1. Admin writes reflection templates (10/year, curated) at /admin/templates
  2. Student completes reflections every 3 weeks (hard cap), working through templates in order
  3. On submit, the app runs synthesis via Anthropic to generate a SignalProfile (interest clusters,
  character signals, trajectory shifts, breadth score)
  4. Student sees their signals on the home page and a career/education map of nodes personalized to
  their profile
