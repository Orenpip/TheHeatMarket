What COMPASS Is

Student career exploration platform. High schoolers complete structured reflection sessions every 3 weeks → AI (Claude + Voyage AI) synthesizes responses into "signal profiles" (interest clusters, character signals, trajectory shifts, breadth score, compressed summary) → counselors see filtered dashboards. A pgvector-powered map surfaces personalized career/major recommendations.
