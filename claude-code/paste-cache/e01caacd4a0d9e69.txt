Project: Compass                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  What it is: A student self-reflection platform. High schoolers complete structured reflection sessions every 3 weeks, an AI synthesizes their responses into a "signal profile" (interest clusters,       
  breadth score, trajectory patterns), and counselors get a dashboard view of their students. There's also a personalized career/major map driven by pgvector similarity search.                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Stack: Next.js 14 (app router) + Turborepo monorepo. Clerk for auth (student / counselor / admin roles). Supabase PostgreSQL + Prisma ORM. Anthropic (claude-sonnet-4-6) for synthesis, Voyage AI for
  embeddings. QStash for background jobs. Upstash Redis for caching. FastAPI Python service for AI work. React Native mobile app (apps/mobile) not touched.
